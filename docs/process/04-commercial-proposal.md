## 5. Terms & Conditions

1. This proposal is valid for 30 days from the date of issue.
2. Government fees are estimates and may vary based on actual charges at the time of processing.
3. WTP fees are fixed for the agreed scope. Any additional services will be quoted separately.
4. Refund policy: WTP fees are non-refundable after work begins. Government fees are refundable only if not yet submitted.
5. Timeline estimates assume timely document submission by the client. Delays in documentation may extend the timeline.
6. WTP reserves the right to pause or terminate the engagement if new risk factors are identified during execution.
