# Retainer Support — Ongoing Services

Post-delivery subscription model for continuous client management. Applicable after Level 2+ engagement completion.

---

## Retainer Tiers

### Basic Retainer — $1,500/month

| Service | Frequency |
|---------|-----------|
| Annual visa renewal coordination | Annual |
| Trade license renewal | Annual |
| Emirates ID renewal | As needed |
| Ad-hoc advisory (email/call) | Up to 2 hours/month |
| CRM monitoring & status updates | Monthly |

**Best for:** L1/L2 clients with simple structures, no employees.

---

### Standard Retainer — $3,000/month

| Service | Frequency |
|---------|-----------|
| Everything in Basic | — |
| Accounting & bookkeeping | Monthly |
| VAT filing | Quarterly |
| Corporate tax filing | Annual |
| Employee visa renewals (up to 3) | As needed |
| Quarterly review call with partner/client | Quarterly |
| Bank relationship management | Ongoing |

**Best for:** L2 clients with active businesses, 1–5 employees.

---

### Premium Retainer — $5,000/month

| Service | Frequency |
|---------|-----------|
| Everything in Standard | — |
| Employee visa processing (up to 10) | As needed |
| Dedicated account manager | Ongoing |
| Wealth management advisory | Quarterly |
| Real estate portfolio monitoring | Quarterly |
| DIFC Foundation administration | Annual |
| Will updates & estate planning | Annual |
| Priority response (< 4 hours) | Ongoing |
| Monthly strategic review | Monthly |

**Best for:** L3 clients with complex structures, multiple entities, family office needs.

---

## What Retainer Covers vs. What's Billed Separately

| Included in Retainer | Billed Separately |
|---------------------|-------------------|
| Coordination & management | Government fees (pass-through) |
| Advisory time (within limits) | New company formations |
| Document preparation | Additional bank accounts |
| Renewal processing | Real estate transactions |
| Status reporting | Litigation / legal disputes |
| Communication with authorities | Audit & tax investigation support |

---

## Retainer Agreement Terms

1. **Minimum commitment:** 6 months
2. **Billing:** Monthly, due on the 1st
3. **Unused hours:** Do not roll over
4. **Scope changes:** Require written agreement
5. **Cancellation:** 30-day written notice after minimum period
6. **Government fees:** Invoiced separately at actual cost (no markup)

---

## Partner Revenue from Retainer

Partners receive a recurring share of retainer revenue for clients they referred:

| Retainer Tier | Monthly Revenue | Partner Share (indicative) |
|---------------|----------------|---------------------------|
| Basic | $1,500 | $150/month |
| Standard | $3,000 | $300/month |
| Premium | $5,000 | $500/month |

**Annual partner revenue from one Premium retainer client:** $6,000/year recurring.
