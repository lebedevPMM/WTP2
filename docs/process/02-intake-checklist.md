# Intake Checklist — KYC Light

Structured pre-screen questionnaire. Completed by partner or client before engagement. This is the primary data input for risk classification and banking scenario selection.

---

## Section 1: Purpose of Engagement

- What is the primary goal of your UAE engagement?
  - [ ] Company formation + corporate banking
  - [ ] Personal banking + residency
  - [ ] Full relocation (family + business)
  - [ ] Asset protection / wealth structuring
  - [ ] Tax residency optimization
  - [ ] Other: ___________

- What is the expected timeline?
  - [ ] Urgent (< 1 month)
  - [ ] Standard (1–3 months)
  - [ ] Planning phase (3–6 months)

## Instructions for Partners

1. Complete all **Required** fields — optional fields help us refine the scenario
2. Attach: passport copy (main page), SOF documentation (bank statements or accountant letter)
3. Submit via: email to partner@wtp.uae or via the website form at /submit-case
4. Expected turnaround: risk verdict within **5–7 business days** of complete submission
5. Incomplete submissions will be returned with a request for missing items
